What are the advantages of Bootstrap?

1. Responsive Design: Easily create responsive websites that adapt to different screen sizes.

2. Time-Saving: Ready-to-use components and templates speed up development.

3. Consistency: Maintain a consistent and professional design across your website.

4. Cross-Browser Compatibility: Ensure your website works well on all modern browsers.

5. Customizable and Extensible: Customize and extend Bootstrap to fit your design needs.

6. Community and Documentation: Access a large community and well-documented resources for support. These advantages make Bootstrap a popular choice for web development.
